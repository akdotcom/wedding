The King of Prussia Mall is the largest shopping mall on the east coast, if you're into that sort of thing.

Valley Forge National Park is a very pretty national park about half an hour away from Normandy Farm. It's the same Valley Forge that George Washington stayed with his army all winter during the revolutionary war, so it's interesting to history buffs and nature lovers.
